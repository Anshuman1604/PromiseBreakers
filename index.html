It may have been created officially on 1st June 2019 but the underlying friendship had started a long way back.

What are we?

PB, an acronym for "Promise Breakers" is the association of 4 bizarre personas who happen to be completely different from each other. However, these differences were overpowered by the commonality they hold whenever it comes to dealing with emotions, perceiving certain situations, or judging persons. Before it's formation, there weren't any regular meet-ups or discussions among the members. With the first talk of the group being about an unnatural relationship, we started sharing almost everything with each other. Be it be waiting for others after classes or hanging out irrespective of the deadlines and time of the day, we started prioritizing their bond over a number of things. Numerous fights and arguments couldn't shake our bond even a bit and we just ultimately end up pulling each other's leg and caring more for each other. Even on being called Pervert, the members don't back out to Make new partners each sem while working on the project Gas solutions and Playing FIFA in the free time. We Ignore each other's mistakes and continue loving each other No Matter what or else it's a matter of Lajjya. We will undoubtedly prove that our friendship will remain young forever even when we turn Buddha-Buddhi.
